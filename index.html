The Program

From Story to Screen

A guided workflow for young creators who want to master the full pipeline of cinematic AI filmmaking. Students move from raw idea to finished visual output — building mythic worlds, Value driven United Nations SDG aligned narratives, and pitch-ready assets along the way.

  • 🎬 Story & Script Mythology-driven narratives and Value driven United Nations SDG aligned impact stories. Students learn to build worlds that matter.
  • 🎨 Characters & Visuals Concept art, character design, and visual worldbuilding using generative AI tools. Students also learn how to create digital avatars for their projects.
  • 🎙️ Voice & Sound Voiceover production, sound design, and cinematic audio layering.
  • ✂️ Animation & Editing Full post-production pipeline using Kling, Higgsfield and systematic scene organisation.

What Students Build

Every session is a production milestone. By the end of the program, each student owns a complete portfolio package: a cinematic AI animation short film, a reel pack, a certificate of completion, and a cinematic pitch deck ready for festival submission or investor presentation.

Output is not theoretical. It is exhibition-ready, competition-ready, and pitch-ready.

Festival & Career Support

Students can submit their finished films to international and regional film festivals. We provide full support to start their journey — from festival strategy and submission guidance to portfolio polish and pitch preparation. Graduation is the beginning, not the end.

Six months of post-program guidance included. Small cohorts. High touch. Real outcomes.
